The Otherside podcast with Rohan Bopanna

16 Dec 2023

Playing a sport you enjoy is one of the most accessible ways to be physically and mentally fit. Doing well in sports is also a great way to build on national identity and pride. If more Indians have to play a sport & be healthier, maybe those who have played sports at the highest levels are best placed to popularize sports.

It is fantastic to see sportsmen like Rohan Bopanna sponsoring other athletes, running academies and investing in sports businesses. Thanks, Rohan, for doing The Otherside podcast with Dilip and spending time with the Rainmatter Health team.
