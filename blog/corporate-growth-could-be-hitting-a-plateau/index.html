Corporate growth could be hitting a plateau

06 Nov 2024

Corporate growth, in both the public and private markets, seems to be plateauing across most sectors. Even in the private markets, in most cases, valuations in the earlier funding rounds were already far over the fundamental value or expected growth of startups, which is why there’s hardly any news about funding for later-stage startups.
