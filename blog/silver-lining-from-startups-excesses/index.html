Silver lining from startups excesses

25 Nov 2022

The silver lining of the startup world’s excesses is that, for the first time in India, there are so many aspiring to be entrepreneurs. Almost like how people want IIT, IIM, Civil Services, etc. More people building for India, in India is the only way we can get to $5 trillion+.
