Introducing Zing! by Zerodha

25 Oct 2023

Right from day 1 of Zerodha, we’ve spent a significant amount of time and effort to educate people about finance through Zerodha Varsity, Z-Connect, Tradingqna, etc. Since video was becoming the dominant way people consumed information, we also launched Varsity videos. Along the way, we’ve been debating whether all this content we were publishing appealed to younger audiences. We’re launching a new YouTube channel called Zing! by Zerodha in partnership with Prateek and Learnapp where we make finance a little more fun. Here’s the first video is on {De-influencing](https://youtu.be/Kgigwie_XZc).
