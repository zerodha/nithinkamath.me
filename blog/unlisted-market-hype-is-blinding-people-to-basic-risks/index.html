Unlisted market hype is blinding people to basic risks

28 Nov 2025

Given how hot the IPO market is, I’m hearing some phenomenally stupid stories from the unlisted market. People are blindly punting on so-called “pre-IPO” companies hoping they’ll make bigger gains than during the actual IPO.

I honestly didn’t expect the unlisted share space to become this popular. Colleagues showed me a platform sending WhatsApp blasts pushing this stuff. It’s kind of crazy what’s happening out there.
