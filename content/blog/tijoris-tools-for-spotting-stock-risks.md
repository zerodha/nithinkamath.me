Indian investors keep getting regular reminders that ignoring corporate governance can come back to bite them in spectacular fashion. A bad stock pick and poor position sizing can wipe out a big chunk of your portfolio.

Avoiding the worst of the worst stocks is perhaps more important than choosing the best ones. [Tijori](https://tijorifinance.com) has two useful features that can help you screen out terrible stocks.

On [Tijoristack](https://tijoristack.ai), you can generate a Risk Probe Report that shows you all the risks, red flags, and vulnerabilities you should watch out for. This is a detailed report that analyzes all the company financials and disclosures.

The second is the forensics tab on their site that gives you a broad overview of things you should dig deeper into.
